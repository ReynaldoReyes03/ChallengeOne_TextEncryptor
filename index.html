Text encryption helps you keep your messages safe. You can encrypt and decrypt the messages you enter in the main text area.

Steps of use

  • Enter your text in the main text area. This text is only valid if it is in lowercase and does not contain any other symbols or special characters. If you insert any characters that are not valid, you will be notified.
  • Click some button, you can choose to encrypt or decrypt.
  • Then your text will appear in another text area with the option you have chosen in the previous step.

You have buttons to

  • Paste the text you have on your clipboard.
  • Clear the main text area.
  • Swap the text you have in the encrypted text area to the main text area.
  • Reset the text cipher to its original state.
  • Copy the text that you have encrypted or decrypted.

Finally, at the bottom, you have the footer with the creator's copyright and all his social media links. You can go and help him be more recognized and support him.
